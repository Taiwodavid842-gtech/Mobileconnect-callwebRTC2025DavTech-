How It Works

Get Your Peer ID

When you load the page, you'll automatically be assigned a unique Peer ID that identifies your device.

Share Your ID

Share your Peer ID with the person you want to connect with. They'll need to enter it to establish a connection.

Connect to Peer

Enter their Peer ID in the connection field and click "Connect" to establish a secure peer-to-peer connection.

Start Communicating

Once connected, you'll see each other's video streams and can start your conversation.

Share Files

Select any file and click "Send File" to transfer it directly to the other peer without any server involvement.
